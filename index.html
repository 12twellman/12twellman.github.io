Hiking and the great outdoors of New Hampshire

Did you know that hiking is not only great exercise! but can reduce anxiety and stress? Go take a hike! Read below for the physical and mental health benefits of hiking!

  • Lowers your risk for heart disease.
  • Improves your blood pressure and blood sugar levels.
  • Boosts your bone density, since walking is a weight-bearing exercise.
  • It strengthens your core, glutes, quadriceps, hamstrings, and the muscles in your hips and lower legs!
  • It can even boost your mood! You'll be happier with less stress and anxiety!

New Hampshire has such a large variety of outdoor, or seasonal activities, that most people that come here can find something that fits their interest and ability.

Although hiking is a major theme in the state, there are many other things to do, such as:

  • Biking
  • Skiing, both on snowy mountain slopes, but also on the many lakes in the state.
  • Sun Bathing on sand covered beaches.
  • Fishing
  • and so much more!
