Lakes, Rivers and the Ocean

The Lakes Region

New hampshire is well known for it's Beautiful Lakes region. The lake most people will know is Lake Winnipesaukee, shown here on a vintage map. Every year thousands of people visit here to swin, sail, fish, or enjoy one of the many cruises.

The Seacoast

Even though the coast line of NH is short, it has plenty to do. From historic Portsmouth, to the sands of Hampton Beach and the Isles of Shoals. Hampton beach is quite a happening place in the summer months, with a bunch of shops, resturants and bars. There is even a concert venue!
