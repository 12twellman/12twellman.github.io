Fire Towers of New Hampshire

Part of the history of New Hampshire is the vast network of firetowers that are placed accross the state. While many of them have either fallen down, or otherwise destroyed, many are still in active service. Just like the AMC 4000 Footers club there is a Merit badge or sorts that can be earned by visiting all of the Fire towers in the state.
