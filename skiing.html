Skiing and Snowboarding in NH

New Hampshire residents are blessed with so many options when it comes to recreation in nature. With all of the mountains that exist in NH, many have been laden with trails for skiing, Snowboarding and tubing. Some of the bigger resort locations also have a range of activites to do during the "off" season, such as downhill mountain biking, and even golfing.

I, myself am a skiier, Vs. being a snowboarder. I like to go fast, but I also enjoy a good view when I see one. I'd like to share some of the places that I enjoyed going to over the past few years.

Waterville Valey

Located in central NH and on a NH 4000 footer, Watervill valey has allot to like. Plenty of slick, fast trails for us speed seekers, but also a great lookout towards neighboring mountain ridges.

Check them out here!

Loon Mountain

Loon mountain is one of the larger mountains in NH, with a maximum vertical drop of 2100 Feet. I like this mountain because it offers a good variety of terrain, usually gets plenty of natural snow, and is right off the highway.

www.loonmtn.com

Mount Sunapee

Mount Sunapee, located in western NH is a slighly smaller resort, but as it is closer to home it's nice to goto when I don't want to drive for hours. They also have a waffle hut at the base of the mountain which makes for a delicious pick-me-up after a day of skiing. YUM!
